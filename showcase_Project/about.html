About US

Welcome to Lush Activewear, your number one source for all your activewear. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of sports and exercise apparel, with a focus on inclusivity, accessibility, and comfertbility. Founded in 2022 by Chelsea Otto, Talia Hendricks, and Elizabeth Kakola.

Lush Activewear has come a long way from its beginnings in Eduvos idea room. When the founders first started out, their passion for sports, exercise, and inclusivity in woman's clothing drove them to start this project and quit their day jobs so that Lush Activewear can offer you comfortable activewear made specifically for women. We now serve customers all over South Africa and Namibia and are thrilled that we're able to turn our passion into our own website. All body types can be accommodated by the wide range of products available We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. .
